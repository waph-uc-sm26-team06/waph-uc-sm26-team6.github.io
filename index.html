About the Project

Our project, named textual healing, is a web based messaging system directed towards the college student. Our goal is to make a platform that seamlessly integrates group chats and direct messages. This is going to be designed for students/ teams that need to stay connected but do not want their communication to get lost in the mess. The end product will be a application that is secure, easy to use and helpful to the user. We will apply Scrum principles, Github collaboration, and modern software engineering workflows.
